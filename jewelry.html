Paw Print or Nose Print Jewelry

We offer several styles of .925 sterling, 14k yellow gold and white gold paw print or nose print jewelry. This beautiful beloved keepsake jewelry is made from the impression of your pet's paw print or nose print. We must know ahead of the cremation process to make the impression of your pet's paw print or nose print. Every print is stored in a secure electronic file to allow you to order additional pieces of jewelry at any time.  Due to the price of solid gold and .925 silver constantly changing, the prices included in the product descriptions are only an estimate and are subject to change. 

As you view our selections, we encourage you to call or email if you have any questions about these products, or if we can be of service to you in any way at all.
