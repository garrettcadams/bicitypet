In your time of need...

A pet’s death may be just as difficult to accept as the death of any loved one and it is natural to grieve just as we would the loss of a close relative or friend.  It is natural to cry, to talk about, and to remember the wonderful, happy times you shared with your pet.

Remembering the years of love and care given to our pets and the lifetime of companionship and devotion returned, we cherish their memories long after their brief lives have ended. It is proper that we give some thought to our pet’s final arrangements.

We offer a number of personalized services designed to meet your specific needs.  Please feel free to contact us with any questions. 

Assisting you in taking care of your pet means a great deal to us.

We are always available to answer any questions you may have about our services and products.

About us...

Owned and operated by The Jordan's, Bi City Pet Cemetery and Crematory has proudly served pets and pet owners since 1990.  As a family, we have enjoyed many personal family pets and as each of you, the loss and heartache of losing that special part of our family.

We are an active member of the International Association of Pet Cemeteries and Crematories.

Our Pet Crematory is located just off the J.R. Allen Parkway in the Columbus, Georgia and Phenix City, Alabama area. Our location is just south of LaGrange, Pine Mountain, Cataula and West Point, Georgia; and East of Auburn, Opelika, Valley and Lanett, Alabama.

Our garden concept cemetery is located in rural Phenix City, Alabama, on Sanfort Road. 
We serve Columbus Georgia and the surrounding areas.
